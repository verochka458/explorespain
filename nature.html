Spain's Diverse Landscapes

Spain offers a unique variety of natural landscapes, from the towering Pyrenees to the volcanic Canary Islands. The Iberian Peninsula is blessed with everything from fertile valleys and rugged mountains to sweeping coastlines.

National Parks and Natural Wonders

There are over 15 national parks in Spain, each showcasing the country's diverse ecosystems. Whether it's the serene Doñana wetlands or the dramatic cliffs of Picos de Europa, Spain's nature captivates at every turn.

Sierra Nevada

The Sierra Nevada National Park is a natural treasure located in southern Spain, famous for its breathtaking mountains and biodiversity.

Doñana National Park

Known for its marshlands, Doñana is a sanctuary for migratory birds and an exceptional wildlife habitat in Spain.

Tenerife

The island of Tenerife offers unique volcanic landscapes, including the famous Mount Teide, the highest peak in Spain.
